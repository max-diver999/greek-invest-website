## What should foreign buyers know about law 5100/2024: 120m² Rule, STR Ban and 2025 Approval Data?


The 120m² single-property rule. The qualifying asset must be one registered property with at least 120 square metres of usable living area on a single title deed. Marketing figures that include terraces, parking bays or communal corridors do not satisfy the test. A civil engineer certificate tied to the Electronic Building Identity is the authoritative source.

The short-term rental ban. A property registered as the Golden Visa qualifying investment cannot simultaneously hold a GNTO/EOT short-term rental licence. Operating unlicensed Airbnb-style lets risks tourism-law fines and jeopardises permit compliance. Long-term residential leases of 12 months or more remain permitted.

Programme volume. Greece granted 8,879 new Golden Visa approvals in 2025, reflecting sustained demand after Spain closed its property route. Processing backlogs at the Ministry of Migration remain the main timeline risk, not the legal eligibility of Rhodes itself.
