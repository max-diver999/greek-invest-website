**Quick answer:** Corfu qualifies for the Greece Golden Visa at the **€400,000 regional tier** under Law 5100/2024. You need one residential property with at least 120 square metres of usable area on a single title deed. Corfu is not in the €800,000 prime zone that covers Attica, Thessaloniki, Mykonos, and Santorini. Short-term tourist rental on the qualifying asset is banned; long-term leases are allowed. At typical asking prices of €2,000 to €3,500 per square metre, €400,000 delivers a compliant villa or large apartment in most Corfu submarkets.

Corfu attracts British, German, and Israeli buyers who want Ionian lifestyle with EU residency at the regional €400,000 threshold rather than Attica's €800,000 floor. The island combines UNESCO-listed Corfu Town, international school options, and a direct flight network that supports owner-use and long-term tenant demand.

This guide maps Golden Visa rules on Corfu, submarket pricing, due diligence, rental restrictions, and how Corfu compares with other €400,000 regional options.

## Corfu Submarkets: Town, Northeast Coast, and Inland

### Corfu Town (Kerkyra)

Corfu Town offers the deepest resale market on the island. Venetian architecture, port connectivity, and year-round services support long-term tenants including professionals and university-linked renters.

Typical asking prices run €2,200 to €3,500 per square metre for renovated stock near the historic centre. A €400,000 budget buys roughly 115 to 180 square metres depending on condition — always confirm usable area on the engineer certificate, not marketed gross figures including terraces.

### Northeast coast (Kassiopi, Barbati, Dassia)

Premium sea-view villas dominate foreign buyer interest. Prices often reach €3,000 to €4,500 per square metre on prime plots. €400,000 may buy less interior space here; verify 120 square metre compliance before deposit.

### Central and south Corfu

Inland and southern villages trade lower at €1,600 to €2,200 per square metre. Buyers prioritising space over prestige often find 140 to 220 square metre homes within budget with renovation headroom.

## The 120 m² Rule on Corfu Stock

Law 5100/2024 requires usable living area on one cadastral title. Open terraces, parking bays, and storage rooms typically do not count toward the 120 square metre minimum.

**Common Corfu pitfalls:** split ownership in old Venetian buildings; annexes on separate titles; pool houses counted in marketing brochures but not on building permits.

Run the [Golden Visa property due diligence checklist](/guides/golden-visa-property-due-diligence-checklist/) with a licensed engineer before any binding offer.

## Off-Plan vs Resale on Corfu

Resale neoclassical apartments in Corfu Town often carry inheritance co-ownership histories requiring months of notary coordination. Off-plan and newly completed villas in northeast developments offer cleaner title but less price negotiation room.

| Factor | Resale Corfu Town | New build northeast |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Title complexity | Higher in old town | Lower with developer escrow |
| Price negotiation | Possible on aged stock | Fixed price lists common |
| 120 m² verification | Engineer essential on pre-1983 stock | Building permit confirms area |
| Timeline to deed | 10–16 weeks if clean | Tied to construction stage |
| Golden Visa fit | Strong if engineer clears area | Strong if occupation certificate issued |

Off-plan buyers should read [off-plan property Greece guide](/guides/off-plan-property-greece-guide/) alongside Golden Visa counsel. Never pay final balance without engineer confirmation that usable area on the deed will meet 120 square metres.

**Step 1:** Obtain Greek AFM tax ID and open a bank account before binding deposit.

## Rental Strategy: LTR Only on the Qualifying Asset

Golden Visa holders may lease long-term residential tenants on the qualifying property. Platforms such as Airbnb and Booking.com are prohibited on that asset while the permit is active.

Gross long-term yields often fall in the four to five percent range before ENFIA, management, and void periods. See [Greece rental yield guide](/guides/greece-rental-yield-guide/) and [no short-term rental rule](/guides/greece-golden-visa-no-short-term-rental/).

## Red Flags and Insider Tips

**Red flags:** co-ownership without all owners signing; unclear cadastre boundaries; GNTO licence history on the unit while marketed for Golden Visa; payment trail not matching Circular 1/2026 banking rules.

**Insider tip:** Corfu Town liquidity beats village prestige for exit planning. If you will not live on the island full time, prioritise tenant depth over sea-view Instagram listings.

**Border zone check:** confirm with counsel that the plot is not misclassified near restricted mainland zones when agents market "Epirus-Corfu packages."

## ENFIA and Holding Costs on Corfu

Non-resident owners pay ENFIA property tax annually regardless of Golden Visa status. Coastal plots with large land footprints carry higher ENFIA bands than compact Corfu Town apartments. Budget €800 to €2,500 per year depending on zone and declared value — your lawyer estimates from the E9 filing after purchase.

Community charges in managed northeast developments add €100 to €400 monthly for pools, gardens, and security. Factor these into five-year holding models alongside [cheapest Golden Visa routes](/guides/cheapest-golden-visa-property-greece-2026/) comparisons when Corfu competes with mainland Peloponnese stock. Request ENFIA estimate before deposit so closing costs do not surprise your all-in budget. Keep six months of ENFIA and community charges in escrow after deed day if you will not visit Corfu monthly to pay utility bills in person.
