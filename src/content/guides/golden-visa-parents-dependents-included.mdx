## Which Parents Qualify and How Many Permits You Can Hold


Greece Golden Visa allows up to four parent permits from one qualifying property investment under Law 5100/2024, covering the investor's mother and father plus the spouse's mother and father with no age limit and no financial dependency test on elderly applicants.

The family definition under Law 5100/2024 covers four parent categories:

| Parent category | Qualifying link | Age or dependency test |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Investor's mother | Birth certificate | None |
| Investor's father | Birth certificate | None |
| Spouse's mother | Marriage certificate + spouse birth cert | None |
| Spouse's father | Marriage certificate + spouse birth cert | None |

A household with two adults, two minor children, and four living parents can therefore hold up to eight permits from one €400,000 Crete purchase or one €800,000 Athens purchase. The investment threshold does not rise with family size. Only administrative fees scale per person.

Parents are permit holders, not co-investors. They hold no ownership stake in the qualifying property unless separately added to the title deed for estate planning reasons, which is uncommon and requires distinct legal advice.

## What should foreign buyers know about biometrics, Mobility, and Practical Planning for Older Parents?

Schengen-wide travel before card issuance is restricted compared with the full permit. Do not book extended European travel for parents until your lawyer confirms which document they hold.
