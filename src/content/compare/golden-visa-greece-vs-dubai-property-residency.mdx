UAE qualifying thresholds vary by emirate and visa route, often sitting below Greece prime tiers when Dubai off-plan stock meets developer eligibility rules.

Greece tiers under Law 5100/2024:

| Zone | Minimum | Notes |

UAE thresholds change with federal and emirate policy. Dubai off-plan and ready property routes have historically sat below Greece prime tier totals but require developer eligibility and mortgage rules verification.

## Tax and Residence Planning

UAE individual income tax is generally not applied at personal level, while Greece taxes Greek-source income, imposes ENFIA on property at annual rates tied to cadastre value, and offers optional incentive regimes for qualifying new tax residents under separate day-count tests that often require under 183 days in Greece.

Many UAE-resident investors maintain Greek Golden Visa property without becoming Greek tax residents. Days present, centre of vital interests, and CRS reporting determine outcomes. Never select a jurisdiction from marketing brochures alone.
