Agencies quote six months. Lawyers quote eighteen. Both can be right depending on which phase they measure and whether the property file is clean. This guide gives three scenario timelines, a month-by-month calendar, and the delay triggers that separate a twelve-month best case from an eighteen-month problem file. For the official phase breakdown, see the companion [Golden Visa processing time guide](/guides/greece-golden-visa-timeline-application-2026/).

## Three Scenarios: Best, Typical, and Problem Files

| Scenario | Total months | Purchase phase | Permit phase | Profile |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Best case | 12 | 8 weeks | 8 months | Clean resale, single applicant, Athens lawyer, complete Circular 1/2026 file |
| Typical | 15 | 10 weeks | 11 months | Family of four, regional €400K property, standard backlog |
| Problem file | 18–24 | 16–24 weeks | 14–18 months | Title legalisation, wrong tier corrected late, bank trail gaps |

Greece approved 8,879 Golden Visa permits in 2025, a 95 percent year-on-year increase, while approximately 11,553 applications remained pending entering 2026. Higher throughput did not eliminate queue time. New filings continue to arrive faster than historical averages, so conservative planning beats optimistic marketing.

## Month-by-Month Calendar From Offer to Permit Card

### Months 1–2: Search, lawyer, and offer

- Instruct independent Greek lawyer before deposit
- Verify Golden Visa tier, 120 square metre usable area, single deed
- Sign preliminary agreement and pay deposit (typically 10 percent)
- Open AFM and Greek bank account in parallel

Link: [making an offer on Greek property](/guides/making-offer-greek-property-step-by-step/) and [buying as a foreigner](/guides/buy-property-greece-foreigner/).

### Months 2–4: Due diligence and deed

- Title search, cadastre check, ENFIA clearance, engineer certificate if needed
- Pay 3.09 percent transfer tax
- Sign final notarial deed and register at land registry
- File permit application within 60 days of registration (Circular 1/2026)

### Months 4–5: Biometrics and bridging document

- All family members attend biometric appointments in Greece
- Receive application receipt or bridging confirmation
- Maintain valid health insurance for every applicant

### Months 5–15: Permit processing queue

- Ministry of Migration reviews investment compliance
- Authorities may request supplemental bank transfer proof or updated registry extract
- Five-year residence permit card issued upon approval

## Family Applications: Where Time Multiplies

Single applicants with one lawyer and one biometric appointment sit at the fast end of the typical band. Families with four parents, two children, and two adults may hold eight permits from one investment but must assemble eight document sets, eight insurance policies, and eight biometric slots.

| Family size | Document assembly | Biometric scheduling | Typical permit slip vs single |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 applicant | 2–3 weeks | 1 day | Baseline |
| 2 adults | 4–5 weeks | 1–2 days | +2–4 weeks |
| 2 adults + 2 children | 6–8 weeks | 2–3 days | +4–6 weeks |
| Extended family (8 permits) | 10–14 weeks | 3–5 days | +6–10 weeks |

Submitting all members together still beats staggered dependent applications because property documentation is compiled once.

## Remote Buyers and Power of Attorney

Remote purchase is standard but timing fails when administrative steps are sequenced wrong. Correct order:

1. Grant POA to Greek lawyer (allow 1–3 weeks for notarisation and apostille)
2. Obtain AFM via lawyer
3. Open Greek bank account
4. Lawyer signs preliminary agreement and deed on buyer's behalf
5. Buyer travels for biometrics after registration

Buyers who find property first and treat AFM and banking as afterthoughts often lose four to eight weeks between deed target and actual signing. The [Golden Visa lawyer guide](/guides/golden-visa-lawyer-greece-complete-guide/) covers POA scope limits.

## What You Can Do in Greece While Waiting

After biometrics and while the file is pending:

- Long-term lease a non-GV property if you need immediate housing (qualifying asset STR still banned)
- Enrol children in schools if bridging status allows legal stay
- Open utility accounts and register with local municipality where permitted
- Do not begin employment without appropriate work authorisation

Schengen travel beyond Greece before card issuance requires lawyer confirmation of which document the holder carries. Tourist visa days do not extend automatically because you own property or have a pending application.

## 2025 Backlog Context and 2026 Outlook

Greece granted 8,879 Golden Visa approvals in 2025 while approximately 11,553 applications remained pending at year end. The approval rate acceleration reflects staffing and digital filing improvements under Circular 1/2026, not instant processing. New applicants in 2026 enter a queue that includes files submitted under both the old €250,000 residential framework and the current Law 5100/2024 tiers, creating heterogeneous processing times inside the same backlog.

Practical implication: your file competes for reviewer attention with incomplete legacy applications that require manual clarification. A complete first submission jumps ahead of resubmission loops even if absolute queue depth remains high. Track status through your lawyer; public portals update slowly and should not drive travel decisions.

## Off-Plan and Conversion Projects: Different Clock

Standard resale timelines above assume an existing deed-ready asset. Off-plan purchases depend on developer completion certificates, engineer sign-off, and sometimes escrow release schedules. Heritage or commercial conversion projects at the €250,000 tier add planning permits and construction phases measured in years, not months.

| Property type | Purchase phase | Permit phase starts |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Resale clean title | 60–90 days | After registration |
| Off-plan with escrow | 12–36 months | After completion certificate |
| Conversion €250K tier | 18–48 months | After habitation certificate |

Buyers choosing off-plan for price reasons should separate property delivery timeline from residency timeline in financial models. The permit clock starts only when the investment qualifies under registered title rules.

## When to Escalate Timeline Risk

Escalate to specialist counsel if any of the following appear during due diligence: inheritance shares among multiple Greek heirs, building constructed before 1983 without final permit, seller is a company in liquidation, property sits within 500 metres of a border zone boundary, or bank compliance requests enhanced due diligence beyond standard source documents. Each condition adds a decision point: proceed with conditions precedent in the preliminary agreement, renegotiate price, or walk away before deposit forfeiture.

Buyers comparing Greece with Portugal or Cyprus residency routes should factor timeline alongside capital. Greece's 2026 backlog is longer than some marketing materials suggest but shorter than first-generation post-2024 tier confusion created. Conservative planning at fifteen months total protects family relocation decisions, school enrolment timing, and lease termination dates in the home country. Add two weeks buffer for public holidays affecting notary and migration office hours.

## Tracking Your File After Submission

Once biometrics are complete, the visible part of the process goes quiet. Your lawyer should provide:

- Application receipt number and submission date (start of the 60-day compliance window proof)
- Expected regional authority based on property location
- List of any supplemental documents requested mid-process
- Bridging document validity dates if issued

Do not book irreversible relocation steps on verbal estimates from agents. Migration offices in Athens, Thessaloniki, and Crete operate on different caseloads. A file submitted in Attica in March 2026 may outpace an identical file submitted in a backlogged island office the same week. The [official phase-by-phase timeline](/guides/greece-golden-visa-timeline-application-2026/) documents legal checkpoints; this page adds scenario planning on top.
